# Implicit Flow

### Step 1: Create a New Application

- Once you are on the Dashboard page, click on `Applications > Applications` available on the left-hand side panel.

<img src="/public/images/deployment/security/auth0/create-new-app-1.png" alt="create-app" />

- Click on `Create Application`.

<img src="/public/images/deployment/security/auth0/create-new-app-2.png" alt="create-app" />

- Enter the Application name.
- Choose an application type and click on `Create`.

<img src="/public/images/deployment/security/auth0/create-new-app-3.png" alt="create-app" />

### Step 2: Where to Find the Credentials

- Navigate to the Settings tab.
- You will find your `Client ID` and `Domain`.

<img src="/public/images/deployment/security/auth0/credentials.png" alt="credentials" />
